Flowers form an integral part of worship places in India. Since the floral offerings to the deities are considered to be sacred, they are not discarded in the trash bins, but rather are usually flung into the local water bodies or rivers leading to river pollution. “Ark”, a project by Enactus, IIT Roorkee is a collective effort to bring in change, to bring forth a sustainable solution to floral waste disposal.
